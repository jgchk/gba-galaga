Galagus is a game very similar to Galaga. The objective is to score as many points as possible by shooting aliens
that are constantly trying to attack you. If you get hit 3 times, you lose!

Controls:
    SELECT      reset game to title screen
    A           shoot
    LEFT        move left
    RIGHT       move right

Scoring:
    100         shoot an alien that is sitting still
    300         shoot an alien that is diving at you
    -500        letting a diving alien get past you to the bottom of the screen
    -25         cost to shoot 1 bullet
